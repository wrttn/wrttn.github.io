## Ingredients

- 8oz (250g) rice noodles
- 1 pkg tofu
- 2 chicken breasts
- 6 Tbsp peanuts, roasted

- 70 ml tamarind concentrate
- 3 Tbsp. fish sauce
- 3 Tbsp. sugar
- 2 Tbsp. lime juice

- 4 garlic cloves
- 2 eggs
- 1 C bean sprouts
- 2 green onion
- 1/2 Tbsp. roasted chilies
- fresh corriander
- lime wedges
- 8 large shrimps

## Method

- Soak noodles 1 hour
- Slice tofu, fry with soy sauce. Reserve.
- Slice chicken to 1/4" strips. Reserve.
- Broil peanuts. Crush. Reserve.
- Combine: tamarind concentrate, fish sauce, sugar, lime juice. Beat to mix. Reserve.
- Heat oil in pan. Add garlic (30s). Add chicken (2min). Add tofu (1min). Break egg, let fry with out breaking 1-2min. 
- While egg cooks, drain noodles, add to pan. Fry 1min. Add tamarind mix. Fry 2min.
- Add 2/3 peanuts and stir. Add 2/3 sprouts and green onion. Fry 1min. Remove from heat.
- Move noodles to serving plates. Top with peanuts, sprouts, red pepper, corriander. Lime wedges for garnish.
