## Ingredients

- Bacon
- Lamb
- Garlic
- 1.5 C Red wine
- 1 lbs mushrooms
- 4 C beef broth
- 1 Tbsp tomato paste
- 1 lbs mushrooms
- 2 bay leaves
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1.5 lbs yellow potato
- 4 carrots 
- salt / pepper

## Method

- Chop and fry bacon
- Cut, wash, dry and flour/salt lamb. Brown in bacon fat. Set aside.

- Chop onion, saute
- Add garlic with onion
- Add 1.5 C red wine, reduce. Set aside. 

- Saute mushrooms. Set aside.

- In pot:
  - Beef broth
  - Bay leaves
  - Typme
  - Yellow potato
  - Carrots

- Bring to boil. Cover and transfer to oven @ 325F for 1 hour and 45min.
