## Raspberry Jam (2018-11-23)

- 2 C raspberries
- 1.5 Tbsp. sugar
- 1 Tbsp lemon
- orange zest
- 2 Tbsp water
- 1 Tbsp chia seeds
- pinch cinnamon/nutmeg

## Method
- Boil 2min (all but chia seeds)
- Simmer 10min
- Add chia seeds, remove from heat.
