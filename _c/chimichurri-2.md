## Ingredients

- 1 C flat leaf parsley
- 1/4 C fresh oregano leaves only
- 1/3 C chopped red onion or shallot
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/2 C olive oil
- 1/4 C red wine vinegar
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp salt

## Method

- Mince before adding liquids.
